## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Create Your Account
- Click "Sign up" on the welcome screen
- Enter any email and password (stored locally, no verification needed)
- Click "Create Account"

### 2. Option A: Load Demo Data (Recommended for First-Time Users)
If you want to see Attune in action immediately:
1. Go to **Settings** (click the Settings icon in navigation)
2. Scroll down to "Load Demo Data"
3. Click "Load Demo Data" button
4. Navigate to **Today** to see your populated dashboard
5. Explore **Trends** to see charts and AI insights

### 3. Option B: Start Logging Manually
1. From the **Today** screen, click any quick action button:
   - **Log Food** - Describe what you ate (e.g., "veggie burrito with guac")
   - **Log Water** - Tap quick amounts or enter custom ounces
   - **Log Craving** - Get supportive alternatives
   - **Log Movement** - Any movement counts!
   - **Log Sleep** - Rate your sleep quality
   - **Log Stress** - Track your stress level

2. After logging a few entries, check your **Daily Snapshot** card on the home screen

3. After a few days, explore **Trends** to see patterns

## Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: How accurate are the calorie estimates?**  
A: They're intentional rough ranges, not precise measurements. The goal is pattern awareness, not exact tracking.

**Q: Can I edit or delete entries?**  
A: Yes! Go to History, find the entry, and click the trash icon.

**Q: Why isn't my trend chart showing?**  
A: You need a few days of data. Try loading demo data or keep logging!

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Currently, data lives in local storage. You can sign out to clear it.

**Q: Will this work offline?**  
A: Yes! Everything runs locally in your browser.
